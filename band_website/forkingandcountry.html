for KING & COUNTRY

For KING & COUNTRY is a christian pop duo composed of Australian brothers Joel and Luke Smallbone in Nashville, Tennessee, U.S.

In 2012 the brothers were declared by Billboard as one of the „new Artists to Watch“ for 2012.

In 2007 they began performing as their own band after they decided to form a band when Luke graduated from high school.
They began their career as „Joel and Luke“, which changed to „Austoville“, before settling on their current name.
